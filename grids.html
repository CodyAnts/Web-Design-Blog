Grids were the bane of my existance

9th October, 2020

Where to start, grids, grids, grids. So many ways to use them. All confusing. I understand that they were revolutionary and made things infinitely easier for seasoned developers. But they're not simple to pick up when you're just getting started. I think I watched a dozen videos on this topic, all claiming things like "learn CSS Grids in 20 minutes". Yes, the video length may be 20 minutes but the amount of time I spent watching and rewinding it was more on the order of an hour.
